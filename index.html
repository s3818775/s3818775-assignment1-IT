  • Overview

    • “Your Home Doctor” is the application for medical care at home. Nowadays, social science has developed astonishingly, but with that development, people are more susceptible to illness due to the environment and life being turned upside down due to having to make money. This application will use artificial intelligence that looks at the illness information or a x-ray picture of the patient to diagnose what illnesses they get. Moreover, the application will show the best doctor for every single illness that people can schedule for the hospital where the doctor works to have the most accurate appointment. I hope it can take less time for illnessers (patients).

    Most people in the rural area just only have phone numbers or email, that is why the application just registers by phone numbers, email, or facebook, google so on (for choice).     Minimal design is the best way to design an application interface for medical which includes many advantages for the users. First, it is designed for every person and every class, the way applications are made for people who do not have acknowledgement in technologies can easily access.

    Using this the patient’s information that the application can find accurately the specialized doctor of the patient’s illness. Next, the patient can schedule with the hospital to meet the doctor on the day that doctor has time.

    Day by day it records the illness of the patient and uses artificial intelligence to evaluate how the illness will be next which can help the patient understand their health status more clearly.

    Connecting with the smartwatch:
    Nowadays, people tend to use smartwatches a lot which include many health features such as measuring heartbeat, ECG measurement, and so on. It is a very important statistics for the application that it can track the patient and healthy people everyday.

    Connecting with new medical equipment:
